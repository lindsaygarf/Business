Here is a sample schedule of how a day participating in our program would look:

  • 12:00 pm - Kids meet at designated central point in community to be picked up my school bus
  • 1:00 pm - Arrive at jail, get through security and settle into alotted space
  • 2:00 pm - Reunite with moms and eat lunch together
  • 2:30 pm - Set up stations and explain activities (i.e. arts and crafts, interactice music sessions, recreational sports)
  • 3:00 pm - Rotate through stations as a child and mom pair
  • 4:00 pm - Clean up and group reflection on the day's activities
  • 5:00 pm - School bus picks up kids at jail
  • 6:00 pm - Children are met by their guardians at designated central point


We hope to combat these three challenges through our programming:

Maladaptive development caused by parental separation Mother's lack of parenting skills because of this separation Child's poor sense of community and feelings of loneliness
