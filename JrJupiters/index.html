Building Bridges, Mending Hearts

Welcome to Jr. Jupiters!

We are a Boston based organization paving the way towards reconnecting children with their incarcerated mothers. We coordinate with local elementary and middle schools to bring children into the local prisons and jails to help support the relationship between mother and child. We are focused on ensuring that being behind bars does not get in the way of allowing a mother to parent her child.


Our Mission

Our mission is to provide children in early childhood with the opportunity to strengthen relationships with their incarcerated mothers while also providing them with a community that will then aid their mental health and their social emotional development, as well as, supporting incarcerated mothers and reducing re-entry rates in the long run.
